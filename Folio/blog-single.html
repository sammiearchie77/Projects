I'M PASSIONATE ABOUT CODING. 😍

  • by:Samuel Archibong
  • date:March 28, 2020
  • 3 Comments

I love to code because it is usable art.

Just like architecture, which is an art used by people daily and affects their everyday life, so is code. You can create something and people interact wuth, use, touch and work with. That is an amazing feeling 😍

Since code is a usable art, a whole team of people are connected to the practice. From the client to the account managers, and including the business people, finance, and the support team. QA team, designers, product managers and project managers. Code touches every single person in the organization and makes a difference to every single user that touches it (for the good or bad). 😀

Coding is power. Feel it, live it, love it! ... It is the language of the gods.

When I manage to build something that saves people time and prevents them from doing tedious tasks, i know that i am making a difference in that person's life. They now have more time to be with their kids, more time to think up ideas (something computers cannot) and more time to connect to people.
